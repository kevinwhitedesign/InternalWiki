The New Colors of SoHo XI

The SoHo Xi BI Color Palette is built upon the core Infor colors and optimized for data visualization. These eight hues have been selected because they work together to create a cohesive look and fit within the overall vision for SoHo XI and Infor products.

Graphite and Slate
